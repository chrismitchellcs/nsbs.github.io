Transition Bikes

We are proud to be one of the largest Transition Bikes dealers in Canada! Transition is a local company from just south of the border, in Bellingham, WA. They make high end mountain bikes for all types of riding from XC to DH. We are always well stocked in everything Transition offers. Their bikes have been proven by multiple editorial awards in recent years and always guarantee a party in the woods!

Norco Bicycles

Norco is a local brand from Coquitlam, BC that have a broad range of bikes. We have Norco mountain bikes for all riders, from beginners looking to do some trails and city riding, to experts wanting the fastest and most advanced bike you can buy.
