Your local mountain bike experts.

North Shore Bike Shop is proudly serving the mountain bike community since 2007. We are a group of passionate mountain bikers who are also keenly focused on delivering excellent customer service.



It's time for your new dream bike.

We are always well-stocked with youth bikes, dirt jumpers, trail, enduro and downhill bikes. With a passionate crew of cyclists on staff, we have tremendous experience across all disciplines of cycling and are happy to help you get the most out of your riding time. We are proud to be dealers of Transition and Norco, two local brands who continue to push the boundaries and make the best bikes in the industry. Whether you are looking for an entry-level hardtail or the best bike money can buy, you will have options at NSBS.

Transition Bikes

We are proud to be one of the largest Transition Bikes dealers in Canada! Transition is a local company from just south of the border, in Bellingham, WA. They make high end mountain bikes for all types of riding from XC to DH. We are always well stocked in everything Transition offers. Their bikes have been proven by multiple editorial awards in recent years and always guarantee a party in the woods!

Visit Transition

Norco Bicycles

Norco is a local brand from Coquitlam, BC that have a broad range of bikes. We have Norco mountain bikes for all riders, from beginners looking to do some trails and city riding, to experts wanting the fastest and most advanced bike you can buy.
